A Little About Me

Hi there! My name is Jennifer Beem. I was born and raised in a land-locked state where the dirt is red and the sky is big. In college I fell in love with the creative outlet that I found to exist in materials science (specifically polymer engineering), which led me to study chemical engineering. After graduating from the University of Oklahoma I spent three years working in industry where I developed new polymer materials. I then chose to pivot my focus into programming through Epicodus! I love all things outdoor! I cycle, hike, row, rock climb, and play the cello (almost always inside). This website showcases my programming learning journey of syntax and logic!
